Account & Data Deletion

Information about deleting your Might Ampora account

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Might Ampora account directly from the mobile application in just a few simple steps:

  1. Open the Vive app on your device
  2. Tap on the Profile tab in the bottom navigation
  3. Scroll down to the "Preferences & Settings" section
  4. Tap on "Delete Account" (below the Log-out option)
  5. Read the confirmation dialog carefully
  6. Tap "Delete" to confirm permanent account deletion
  7. Your account will be deleted immediately and you'll be logged out

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following information will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number, location)
  • All authentication credentials (Google or phone-based login)
  • Complete activity history (steps, driving data, CO2 savings)
  • Monthly and daily activity summaries
  • All gadget and solar-related data associated with your account
  • Session tokens and refresh tokens
  • Any preferences or saved settings

Note: This deletion is immediate and applies to both the mobile app and our backend servers.

Data Retention & Deletion Timeline

Once you delete your account:

  • Immediate: Your account and all associated data are permanently deleted from our active database
  • Immediate: You are automatically logged out from all devices
  • Immediate: All authentication tokens are invalidated and cannot be used
  • No Recovery: There is no way to recover your account or data after deletion
  • No Backups: We do not retain backup copies of deleted accounts

Important: If you want to use Might Ampora again after deleting your account, you will need to create a completely new account.

Alternative: Just Log Out

If you're not sure about permanent deletion, you can simply log out of your account instead:

  • Your account and data will be preserved
  • You can log back in anytime using the same credentials
  • All your activity history and settings will be waiting for you

To log out: Go to Profile ‚Üí Log-out in the app.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities:

  • Account deletion is processed securely using encrypted authentication
  • Only you can delete your own account
  • The deletion process requires confirmation to prevent accidental deletion
  • All data is permanently removed with no possibility of recovery
  • We comply with data protection regulations including GDPR

Technical Details (For Developers)

Account deletion is handled through our secure API endpoint:

POST /api/v1/users/delete-account

This endpoint requires authentication via refresh token and permanently deletes all user data from our database upon successful verification.
